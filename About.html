About Us

Our Story

TechGadget was founded in 2010, starting as a small computer repair shop in Sydney and has now grown into Australia's leading ICT equipment supplier. Our mission is to help individuals and businesses achieve digital transformation through innovative technology solutions.

After more than a decade of development, we have established a comprehensive supply chain system and a professional technical support team, with service coverage throughout Australia. We have established strategic partnerships with global top brands to ensure we provide customers with the latest and highest quality ICT products.

Our Mission

To empower Australia's digital transformation by providing exceptional ICT products and professional services, making technology accessible to everyone.

Core Values

  • Innovation: Continuously explore new technologies and solutions
  • Integrity: Maintain transparency and honesty in all business activities
  • Excellence: Pursue the highest standards in products and services
  • Collaboration: Grow together with customers and partners
